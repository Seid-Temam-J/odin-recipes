Lobster Rolls

Description

Experience the taste of coastal New England with our classic Lobster Rolls recipe. Perfectly cooked lobster meat, mixed with a touch of mayonnaise, lemon juice, and a hint of seasoning, served in a toasted, buttery roll. This dish is the epitome of summer, bringing you the flavors of the sea in every bite. Whether you're hosting a backyard barbecue, a beach picnic, or simply craving a seafood delight, our Lobster Rolls are sure to impress and satisfy. Dive into this easy-to-follow recipe and enjoy a culinary journey to the shores of Maine

Ingredients

  • Lobster Meat
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lemon juice
  • Celery
  • Chives
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Hot Dog Buns or New England-style Split-top Rolls
  • Lettuce Leaves
